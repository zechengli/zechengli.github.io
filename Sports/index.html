Pole Vaulting

I was also a collegiate athlete when I was 18 at AHNU. In Chinese college sports there are groups A (general students), B (professional athletes), and C (elite athletes). I was in Group A and represented AHNU at the provincial games. I placed 8th in pole vaulting with a jump of 2.9 meters. Interestingly, I was originally training for 800m and 1500m events, but the coach realized I wouldn’t score points there. They switched me to pole vaulting — after just 3 months of training, I managed to score. I was almost cut from the team before that.
